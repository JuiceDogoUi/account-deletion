Account Deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Account"
  4. Tap on "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your choice

Option 2: Contact Support

If you're having trouble deleting your account through the app, you can:

  1. Email us at: talewhisperapp@gmail.com
  2. Include your account details
  3. Request account deletion

We will process your request within 48 hours.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted
  • This action cannot be undone
  • You'll need to create a new account if you want to use the app again

Data Deletion

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data in the following ways:

  1. Send an email to talewhisperapp@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include in your email:
    • Your account email address
    • Any specific data you want to be deleted

What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Personal profile information
  • User-generated content
  • Usage history
  • Any stored preferences or settings

Our Commitment

  • We will respond to your request within 48 hours
  • Data deletion will be completed within 7 business days
  • We will send you confirmation once the deletion is complete
  • We comply with all applicable data protection laws
